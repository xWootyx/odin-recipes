Breadtwists

Delicious and authentic Italian breadsticks, aka Breadtwists.

Ingredients

Dough Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 TBS sugar
  • 1 TBS yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 TSP salt

Other Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter divided in half
  • 2 TSP minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 TSP kosher salt
  • 1 TSP basil
  • 1 TSP oregano

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour and salt. Mix until smooth. Use stand mixer with bread hook.
  3. Cover and let rise for 10 minutes
  4. On a floured surface, roll the dough out into a square. Melt butter and mix in minced garlic. Spread the garlic butter over the whole dough square. Sprinkle with salt.
  5. Fold the square in half and cut the dough into 1-inch strips. Twist each strip and place on a baking sheet. Let rise for 20 minutes.
  6. bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes or until golden brown. While the breadsticks are still warm, melt butter and add minced garlic, basil and oregano. Brush the herb butter over the breadsticks. Top with parmesan.
