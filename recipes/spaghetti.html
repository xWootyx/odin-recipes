Wooty's Fancy Spaghetti

This tasty dish is comprised of the choicest of ingredients. Saucy and rich in bold flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 TBS Olive Oil
  • 1 small onion chopped finely
  • 3 carrots peeled, chopped
  • 1 LB ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 1/2 LB tomatoes, fresh peeled and diced
  • 6 fresh basil leaves chopped
  • 1/2 TSP sugar
  • 1 TSP Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 TSP pepper
  • beef broth, for taste
  • 1 LB spaghetti noodles

Steps

  1. Heat oil on medium heat in a large, deep skillet. Add onion, carrots, and garlic and saute for 1 minute. Add ground beef and pork and continue to cook.
  2. Add tomatoes once meat is brown and onions are soft
  3. After 4-5 Minutes once tomatoes have softened, add basil, sugar, salt and pepper
  4. Simmer for at least 3 hours (and up to 6 hours), stirring occasionally. The longer you simmer, the better. Add beef broth to thin sauce as needed.
  5. In a large pot, cook spaghetti in salted water to al dente, drain completely and return to pot. Add sauce to the spaghetti noodles and toss until noodles are covered.
  6. Serve immediately.
